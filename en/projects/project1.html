IPTV / OTT service

support

An interactive TV service available on the web, mobile apps, and smart TV. Watch your favorite movies, TV shows, and TV shows wherever you have an Internet connection!

This is a resource that was part of the media holding owned by Ukrainian oligarch Rinat Akhmetov, Media Group Ukraine. Also in this holding: 8 TV channels, a full-cycle media agency, a satellite broadcasting operator, 2 production companies, a multimedia news platform - a website and a TV channel, and a cross-platform regional news project.

My part of the work was to perform tasks to support and develop the front-end part. Also, I was responsible for layout of promotional pages and making adjustments to the existing ones.

The service consists of a rich catalog of items, a personal user account, billing, and an online payment service.

The Football section broadcasts the most exciting football events online and the most interesting football highlights in the format of highlights. The standings table allows you to get the most up-to-date rating and statistical data of the matches.

Of course, like every communication resource, there were tasks to layout emails and communication pages.

From July 22 The service has ceased to exist. All the licenses held by Media Group Ukraine were transferred to the state as a result of the Law on Oligarchs. All the company's offices and services on the Internet have been closed, so now only screenshots are provided, not links
