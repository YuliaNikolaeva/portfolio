A website for an OTT service provided to mobile operators.

Layout and support

Favorite programs, football matches online or recorded, if you have a mobile phone with the Internet.

This cooperation has made it possible to create more favorable conditions for the end user to use the OTT service, provided that he or she is already a subscriber of a certain mobile operator (provided that the Internet is available).

My responsibilities were to fulfill deliverables on layout, branding, and making individual client requests for the service.

In the evening of July 22 the service has ceased to exist. All the licenses held by Media Group Ukraine were transferred to the state as a result of the Law on Oligarchs. All the company's offices and services on the Internet have been closed, so now only screenshots are provided, not links
