Ukraine Media Group is a popular sports broadcasting company. The Futbol 1, 2 or 3 channel offers an opportunity to watch matches of the Ukrainian national team and European Cup competitions. The following international and national tournaments were available on the channels: Champions League, Europa League, national team matches, Ukrainian Premier League, Italian Serie A, French Ligue 1, Dutch Redivisie

Cable and satellite subscribers can access these channels by watching Futbol 2 (as well as 1 and 3) on TV as part of their provider's package. Users of any device with Internet access can subscribe to the following services IPTV and OTT service or other online broadcasting platform or mobile operator providing such service.

My tasks on this project were product support and development work, as well as debugging tasks.

From July 22 stopped working TV channels that were part of Media Group Ukraine: Futbol 1, Futbol 2, Futbol 3, Shakhtar TV, Sigma TV, Mariupol TV, Ukraine, Ukraine 24, NLO.TV, Donbass, Indigo TV.
